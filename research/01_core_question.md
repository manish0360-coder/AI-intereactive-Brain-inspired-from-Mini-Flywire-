# Purpose

This document defines the single scientific question that guides all MiniFlyWire research.

Every ontology, hypothesis, experiment, benchmark, mechanism, and implementation must contribute toward answering this question.

If a proposed idea cannot be connected to this question, it does not become part of the research program.

---

# Candidate Core Research Question

What computational mechanisms enable artificial cognitive systems to acquire, organize, refine, and transfer computational knowledge across changing environments and tasks?
