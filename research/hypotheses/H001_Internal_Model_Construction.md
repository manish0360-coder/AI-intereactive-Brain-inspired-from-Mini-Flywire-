# H001 — Internal Model Construction

Status: Draft

Version: v0.1

---

## 1. Scientific Observation

Existing artificial intelligence systems demonstrate increasingly capable behavior, yet there is no experimentally validated methodology explaining how progressively richer internal computational models emerge through interaction with the environment and subsequently support increasingly complex engineering reasoning.

## 2. Research Question

By what computational mechanisms does an artificial cognitive system progressively construct reusable internal models through interaction with its environment, and how do those internal models enable increasingly capable engineering reasoning while satisfying explicit environmental, logical, physical, and task-specific constraints?
